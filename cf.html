College Fashionista Article


College Fashionista interviewed me about my personal style and my involvement with Armour Magazine. Read the full interview and check out some more pictures they took of the outfit I wore for the shoot.


"...the trajectory I want not only Armour, but all college magazines to go on, is to dig into deeper implications of fashion; I want them to not only say, ‘Oh, leather is in this season,’ but to say, ‘Oh, but where does that leather come from?’, to go into deeper issues in fashion — to critique it. Students have the opportunity to rebuild fashion’s infamy through digging deeper into the harder messages of what fashion is."
