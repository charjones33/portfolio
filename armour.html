Armour Magazine


Since Winter 2014 I have been an editor of Armour Magazine, Washington University's student-run, quarterly fashion publicaton. My duties comprise brainstorming and curating written and editorial content, writing and editing articles, styling editorials and layout, handling marketing outreach through our blog, Instagram, and Facebook accounts and assisting in Armour's coverage of third party events. Past examples of my writing include The Resurgence of the Undercut, Pulling it Off and the opposing side of an Op-ed piece concerning Fast Fashion.
